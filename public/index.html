About Our Card Game

Kaya teaches kids ages 8+ the basics of budgeting, saving goals, handling expenses, and making smart investment choices—all through fast-paced, engaging card battles! It’s designed to spark important conversations about money in a zero-risk, fun environment.

Every card draw and decision helps build real-world financial skills. It's playtime with a purpose!

Key Game Mechanics

Goal Setting

Players draw 'Goal Cards'—from buying a new bike to saving for college—and strategically manage income to reach them.

Expense Management

Unexpected 'Bill Cards' (like overdue rent or broken toys) force players to balance their budget and prioritize spending.

Smart Investing

Use 'Investment Cards' to grow your wealth, learning about compound interest and risk vs. reward in a playful way.
