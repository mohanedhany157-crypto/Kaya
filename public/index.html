About Our Card Game

Kaya teaches kids ages 8+ the basics of budgeting, saving goals, handling expenses, and making smart investment choices—all through fast-paced, engaging card battles. It’s designed to spark important conversations about money in a zero-risk, fun environment.

Every card draw and decision helps build real-world financial skills. It's playtime with a purpose!
