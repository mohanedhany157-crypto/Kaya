Game Overview

Kaya is a fast-paced strategy and reaction game where players compete to clear their cards before everyone else. Easy to learn, fun to master.

```

Setup

  • Shuffle all cards well.
  • Deal an equal number of cards to all players.
  • Place the remaining cards in the center as the draw pile.

How to Play

  • Players take turns placing 1 card onto the center pile.
  • When a matching number or symbol appears, players must react quickly depending on the card rules.
  • The slowest player takes the pile.
  • The first player to finish all their cards wins.

Special Cards

  • Match Card: Everyone must slap the pile.
  • Skip Card: Player next to you loses their turn.
  • Reverse Card: Changes the direction of play.
  • Wild Card: Player chooses the next symbol or color.

Winning

The first player who finishes all their cards wins the game.
```
