Official Manual

The Objective

Be the first player to save K500!

Alternatively, play until the deck runs out. The player with the highest total savings wins.

Game Setup

  1. Shuffle: Shuffle the deck thoroughly.
  2. Deal: Deal 7 cards to each player.
  3. Bank Pile: Place all Income Cards (Salary, Side Hustle, etc.) face-up in front of you.
  4. Start: Whoever has the least money starts first!

How to Play Your Turn

1
Draw one card from the deck.
2
Pay or Deflect any consequence (Needs, Wants, Attacks).
3
Play one Action Card into the center pile.

Card Types Breakdown

Income Cards

  • Salary, Side Hustle, Business Profit, Scholarship, Interest on Fixed Deposit.

Need Cards

Essential expenses. Cannot be blocked (except by Bankruptcy).

  • Bills (K50, K100).

Want Cards

Fun expenses you want to avoid!

  • Subscription, Gaming, Credit Card Debt, Theme Park, Online Shopping.
