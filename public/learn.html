Official Manual

The Objective

Be the first player to save K500!

Alternatively, play until the deck runs out. The player with the highest total savings wins.

Game Setup

  1. Shuffle: Shuffle the deck thoroughly.
  2. Deal: Deal **7 cards** to each player.
  3. Bank Pile: Place all **Income Cards** (Salary, Hustle, etc.) face-up in front of you. This is your "Bank Pile".
  4. Start: Whoever has the **least money** in their Bank Pile starts first! Play moves clockwise.

How to Play Your Turn

1
Draw one card from the deck.
2
Pay or Deflect any consequence from the previous player (e.g., Needs, Wants, or Attacks).
3
Play one Action Card from your hand into the center pile.

Card Types Breakdown

Income Cards

Adds money to your savings. Place these face-up.

  • Salary, Hustle, Business Profit, Scholarship, Interest on Fixed Deposit.

Need Cards

Essential expenses. Cannot be blocked (unless you use Bankruptcy).

  • Bills (K50, K100).

Want Cards

Fun expenses you want to avoid paying!

  • Subscription, Gaming, Credit Card Debt, Theme Park, Online Shopping.

Pro Rules & Special Conditions

No Action Cards?

If you have no action cards to play, draw 2 cards. If you still have none, your turn is skipped.

Empty Hand?

If you have 0 cards in hand at the start of your turn, draw 3 cards (2 + 1).

No Change?

If the center pile has no loose change, you must pay with your Income Cards without receiving change back.

Out of Income?

If you cannot pay, you must discard one card from your hand.
