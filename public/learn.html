Official Manual

The Objective

Be the first player to save K500!

Alternatively, play until the deck runs out. The player with the highest total savings wins.

Game Setup

  1. Shuffle: Shuffle the deck thoroughly.
  2. Deal: Deal **7 cards** to each player.
  3. Bank Pile: Place all Income Cards face-up in front of you.
  4. Start: Whoever has the **least money** starts first!

How to Play Your Turn

1
Draw one card.
2
Pay any consequence.
