Game Setup & Rules

Getting Started

Welcome to the game! Before diving in, ensure that all players are ready and understand the basic mechanics. Follow these setup steps carefully to begin a smooth and fair game session.

Setup Instructions

  • Deal the Cards: Each player is dealt **seven (7) cards** from the deck. These cards represent your available resources, actions, and opportunities throughout the game.
  • Income Cards: Every player‚Äôs **Income Cards** (representing their money sources) should be placed face-up in front of them. Tip: For a more unpredictable and exciting experience, you can choose to keep income sources hidden until used.
  • Starting Player: The player with the **lowest total income** becomes the first player. Gameplay proceeds in a clockwise direction, unless a card effect reverses the order later in the game.

Turn Sequence

Each player‚Äôs turn follows the same general structure:

  1. Draw a Card: Begin your turn by drawing one card from the main deck.
  2. Resolve the Centre Card: If there is a card in the centre (from the previous turn), you must either pay the required amount or carry out the card‚Äôs action as stated.
  3. Play a Card from Your Hand: Choose one card from your hand to play into the centre area. The colour of the card determines its type:
    • Needs (Yellow): Represents essential expenses or obligations.
    • Wants (Red): Represents luxury expenses or personal desires.
    • Action Cards (Black, Blue, Orange, Purple): Cards that trigger specific gameplay effects.
