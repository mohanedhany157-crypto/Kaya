Game Setup & Rules

Getting Started

Welcome to the game! Follow these setup steps carefully to begin a smooth and fair game session focused on financial strategy.

Setup Instructions

  • Deal the Cards: Each player is dealt **seven (7) cards** from the deck. These represent your available resources and actions.
  • Income Cards: Every player‚Äôs **Income Cards** (money sources) should be placed face-up in front of them in their Income Zone.
  • Starting Player: The player with the **lowest total income** becomes the first player. Gameplay proceeds in a clockwise direction.

Turn Sequence

Each player‚Äôs turn follows three main steps:

  1. Draw a Card: Begin your turn by drawing one card from the main deck.
  2. Resolve the Centre Card: You must resolve the effect of the card played by the previous player (usually paying the required amount or executing the action).
  3. Play a Card from Your Hand: Choose one card from your hand to play into the centre area. The color determines its type:
    • Needs (Yellow): Essential expenses or obligations.
    • Wants (Red): Luxury expenses or personal desires.
    • Action Cards (Black, Blue, Orange, Purple): Cards that trigger specific gameplay effects.
Special Rule: Efficient Management

If a player runs out of cards in hand at any point, they immediately draw two new cards to reward efficient spending/management.

Winning Conditions

The game ends when one of the following conditions is met:

  • A player reaches RM500 in total savings.
    This player is declared the winner, successfully achieving their primary financial goal first.
  • The draw deck runs out of cards.
    If this occurs, all players count their total savings. The player with the highest amount of money wins the game.
